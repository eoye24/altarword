OBS Setup Guide
STEP 1 — DISPLAY SOURCE
Sources → + → Browser. URL: full path to AltarWord.html with ?mode=display appended. Width: 1920, Height: 1080.
STEP 2 — CONFIDENCE MONITOR
Add a second Browser source with ?mode=confidence. Output to your confidence screen.
STEP 3 — PHONE REMOTE
Run start-server.bat. Your phone URL will appear in the console. Open it in Chrome on your phone (same Wi-Fi required).
